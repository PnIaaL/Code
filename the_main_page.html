楚漆

究竟是什么？

 虽然漆器不是荆州独有，楚式漆器从字面上来看也没有代表着全部的中国漆器。荆州作为春秋战国时楚国的都城所在地， 楚式漆器可以说代表着当时中国漆器技艺的最高水平。同时在今天大量出土的精美楚式漆器，又证明了一个事实，楚式 漆器是中国漆器最重要的一个分支。

而楚式漆器髹饰技艺是一种民间漆器髹饰传统技艺。至春秋战国时期，楚国的漆器制作工艺已达到了极高水平。许多文 物考古专家都以为制作技艺已经失传，事实上，2000多年来，这一独具东方文化特色的荆楚传统技艺，一直在荆沙地区 不断传承，近40年来，荆州地区楚墓中漆器陆续出土，已超过五千余件。

What

is lacquer?And why is it so unique?

Although lacquer is not unique to Jingzhou, Chu-style lacquer does not represent all Chinese lacquer literally. As the capital of Chu in the Spring and Autumn Period and the Warring States Period, Chu-style lacquer can be said to represent the highest level of Chinese lacquer technology at that time. At the same time, a large number of exquisite Chu-style lacquer unearthed today also proves the fact that Chu-style lacquer is the most important branch of Chinese lacquer. And Chu-style lacquer decoration technology is a traditional folk lacquer decoration technology. By the Spring and Autumn Period and the Warring States Period, the lacquer manufacturing technology of Chu has reached a very high level. Many cultural archaeology experts think that the manufacturing technology has been lost. In fact, for more than 2000 years, this unique traditional technology of Jingchu culture has been inherited in Jingsha area. In the past 40 years, more than 5,000 pieces of lacquer have been unearthed from the tombs of Chu in Jingzhou area.
